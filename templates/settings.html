Risk Management Guide

  • Capital: Only trade with money you can afford to lose completely.
  • Per-Trade Risk: Risking 1-2% per trade means you can survive 50-100 consecutive losses.
  • Daily Loss Limit: When hit, stop trading. Emotional trading after losses increases risk.
  • Position Sizing: The system calculates quantity so your stop-loss hits within your risk limit.
  • No Guarantee: The "estimated probability" is a heuristic score, not a guarantee of outcome.
